Why Seth & Leah? you might ask.

The answer maybe: why not? We all often need what we don't have...

Posted by Joja Sibabale on August 24, 2014

You might ask yourself why this story, and why I wrote and continue writting it? Well I never decided to write this story but it just came to me. The answer as to why; well, you too can answer that one, but for me it's based on the fact that opposites attrack.

For so long in the history of the Earth most countries have we been democratized one way or the other, and yet we see few inter-racial relationships. This I believe constitues to the racial divide which stalls the human race to achieving the Ultimate.

Eastern Philosphy speaks of the Yin & Yang, the dualities of life which exist everywhere in this world of ours and these go together and in most cases need each other to thrive. We have Heaven and Earth, male, female, left, right, good and bad...

During the process of writting Seth & Leah I realized this story is a great tool to unite each other (Humanity). To open the vortex towards exploring each others world, as a black young man I was and still am curious about other races and cultures.

Seth & Leah allow us to peek through this vortex of racial divide and takes us into seeing two races being intimate on a deeper levels, going beyond the physical.

The Final Frontier

From each post we are pushing the bounderies of these two young teenagers as they explpore life and love, both with each other and with God.

Love in its purest form is uncoditional and survives whirlwinds, storms, distance, even sin has no power over love. Seth & Leah are a proof of this.

if you saw the soul of your so-called enemy you would probably love it. (-unkown-)

Also this story is about God showing us that He and Jesus the Christ of Nazareth love us and are part of our lives. You will note that Seth & Leah are not your convention Christians or Jews but their connection with Higher Life is unparalled by anything in mordern society. Thus proving Gods unconditional love to us all.

Reaching for the Stars

Seth & Leah also go as far as uniting all forms of Faith but not compromising on the Superiority and Sovereignty of Christinity even over on Judaism. Most religions help us realize many fundemental truths but there can only be one Creator of all things.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.

You are bound to explore extraterristial dimesions and explore worlds beyond common thought, all this in proving love knows no bounds and so does God even Jesus.

This story is intimate and erotic but all is bound within religious and metaphyscial context. Yes you will find spicy language but that is all meant to entertain.
